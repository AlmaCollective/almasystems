# Alma Foundations  
*The Deep Structure Behind a System That Understands the Human Nervous System*

Alma is not built for one industry, one vertical, or one use case.  
It is built around a single fundamental principle:

**The nervous system tells the truth before the mind can interpret it.**

This document describes the core foundations that make Alma versatile,  
hard to categorise, and valuable across multiple domains.

---

## 1. Alma’s Core Insight  
Every emotional state begins as a pattern of micro-signals: breath changes, HRV shifts, tension waves, instability loops, micro-dissonance between intention and action.

The mind perceives these signals *later*.  
Alma aims to detect them *first*.

Alma does not interpret emotions.  
It detects **destabilisation of the nervous system** before it spirals into behaviour.

Alma is **none** of these.

**A real-time clarity layer that operates before emotion becomes behaviour.**

This makes Alma inherently cross-domain:

- personal regulation  
- team cohesion  
- founder resilience  
- crisis prevention  
- system-level emotional stability  
- human-AI interaction  
- organisational health

Alma is not a device.  
Alma is an *operating principle*.

## 3. Alma’s Structure (The Four Layers)

### 1. **Sensing Layer**  
Basic micro-signals (breath, tension, rhythm variability, recovery waves).

### 2. **Interpretation Layer**  
Alma maps these into **“stability vs destabilisation”**, not moods or labels.

### 3. **Clarity Layer**  
A simple cue delivered in real-time:
“regulate now”

No emotion naming.  
No cognitive load.  
Just clarity.

### 4. **Application Layer**  
Different faces of the same system:

- B2C: emotional clarity for individuals  
- B2B: teams, leadership, decision-making  
- B2V: founder resilience  
- B2G: systemic clarity & early detection of collective overload
